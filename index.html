About 1point5

1point5 assists you with social distancing. The app scans for nearby mobile devices and it warns you when a device enters your 1.5 meter parameter. The phone vibrates and a pop-up appears. The pop up contains a graphic that can be shown to the other person. It is a friendly reminder that aims to defuse the situation.


This is a social distancing app and it works using Bluetooth. Devices of family members and so forth can be excluded. The parameter can be adjusted from a minimum of 1.5 meters up to 4 meters, depending on your preference or local regulation. 1point5 aims to help stop the spread of disease. It is not full proof.


Please follow the advice of the World Health Organisation and your local government.
